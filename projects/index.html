TalkToTheHand

At HackTX 2018, my team built a prototype armband that can translate sign-language to speech. We classif gestures on Myo armband to 96% accuracy using a machine learning algorithm (K-nearest neighbor). Then an Android app and Microsoft Azure's Cognitive Services translates the text to speech. Our team won 2nd place for the TeaherTalent challenge to solve a key civic sector problem.
